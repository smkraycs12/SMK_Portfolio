About Me

Lets start with the basics. My name is Shane Kraycs and I am 26 years old. I was born and raised in a small town in the Northern Lower-Peninsula of Michigan called Grayling. I have never lived outside of Grayling for an extended period. Currently I am employed as an emergency dispatcher at the Crawford County Sheriffs Department, however my job also includes dispatching for fire and EMS(emergency medical services). Now I am looking to expand my skill set into web developement. Over the 26 years of my existance I have been lucky and unlucky in many way, through my experiences I have gained much. When is was 10 years old I was diagnosed with a bone cancer called Osteogenic Sarcoma and was very fortunate to survive the year long ordeal. Through this trial I learned many important lessons like the importance of family, the need for a positive attitude, and never giving up. Now I am still trying to write my story.
